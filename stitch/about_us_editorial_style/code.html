A Tradition of Excellence

Founded on the principles of accessibility and academic rigour, NDRK First Grade College began its journey as a visionary project to bring world-class education to the region. From a modest structure with just three departments, we have evolved into a multi-disciplinary hub of innovation.

Our growth has been dictated by the changing needs of the global landscape, yet our roots remain firmly planted in the values of integrity and community service that our founders cherished.
