Common Queries

Admissions

What is the application deadline for the 2024 academic session? expand_more
General applications for the undergraduate program close on August 15th. We recommend early submission to ensure housing priority and eligibility for merit-based scholarships.
menu_book

Academics

Does the college offer credit transfers for online certifications? expand_more
Yes, through our partnership with the Coursera Career Academy, specific certifications are eligible for credit alignment within our elective frameworks. Please consult the Dean's office for the approved list of courses.
How is the faculty-to-student ratio structured? expand_more
We maintain a rigorous 1:20 ratio to ensure personalized mentorship and active engagement in seminar-style learning environments.
payments

Financial Aid

Are there merit-based scholarships available for out-of-state students? expand_more
NDRK offers the 'President's Excellence Grant' which covers up to 40% of tuition for students who maintain a GPA above 3.8 and demonstrate leadership potential, regardless of residency.

Our administrative helpdesk is available Monday through Friday for in-person consultations or digital support regarding any complex queries.
